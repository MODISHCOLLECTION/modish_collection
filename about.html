About Us

At Modish Collection, we specialize in manufacturing and supplying high-quality school uniforms and customized shirts tailored to your needs. Based in Ludhiana, our state-of-the-art facility ensures precision, comfort, and style in every product.

Our team brings together years of experience in textile design, production, and customer service. We believe in delivering not just uniforms, but identity and confidence to every student and professional.

We cater to schools, corporate events, promotional campaigns, and more, offering a variety of fabrics, styles, and printing options. With pan-India delivery and prompt customer support, we ensure seamless service from order to delivery.
